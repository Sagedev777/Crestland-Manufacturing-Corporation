Our Mission

To transform Uganda's agricultural and industrial potential into a globally competitive manufacturing economy by:

  • Vertical Integration: Building end-to-end supply chains from farm-to-fabric-to-fashion to add value to local cotton.
  • Job Creation: Generating thousands of permanent and indirect jobs, uplifting communities through skills training.
  • Food & Economic Security: Combating food insecurity via charitable gardens while reducing trade deficits through domestic production.
  • Public-Private Collaboration: Partnering with government, East African businesses, and global experts to scale sustainable industries.
